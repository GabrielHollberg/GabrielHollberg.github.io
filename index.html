About Me



My name is Gabriel Hollberg, and this is my website!

I am a student at Western Governors University, a FedEx courier, and, above all, a seeker of truth.

I created this website for several reasons.

First, it's one of my passions. Technology has always fascinated me, and the idea of creating something that can be accessed from anywhere in the world with just a few taps on the keyboard was too good to pass up.

Second, I've always thought it would be cool to have a space like this to organize my thoughts and ideas. Hopefully, someday, my mind will be just as organized.

Finally, and these are just a few of my reasons, I'd love for my ideas to be available to my friends (anyone reading this) and family, so those who are interested can also see what goes on inside my head.

This website also serves as an example of my work for any potential future employers.
