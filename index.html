About Me

Hi, my name is Gabriel Hollberg, and this is my website!

I'm a student at Western Governors University, a FedEx courier, and, above all, a seeker of truth.

I created this website for several reasons.

First, it's one of my passions. Technology has always fascinated me, and the idea of creating something that can be accessed from anywhere in the world with just a few taps on the keyboard was just too cool to pass up.

I've always wanted to create a space like this to organize my thoughts and ideas. I'd love for these to be available to my friends and family so those who are interested can see what goes on inside my head.

This website also serves as an example of my work for any potential future employers.
